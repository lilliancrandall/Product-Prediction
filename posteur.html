Posteur

Posture is one of the most important things to a happy life. This proclamation of mine led me to design Posteur, a posture correcting necklace. The idea is to have a capacitative touch sensor on the back of a necklace emblem that sends an alert to a small vibrating device attached to the necklace's clasp. When the touch sensor has not been resting on your chest for a certain amount of time, the clasp will vibrate softly to assure you that you have been slouching and that you need to sit up straight.

Design Process

Posteur is a piece of fashion with function. It quietly reminds you to sit up when you are slouching through a gentle buzz. While this project is still in the prototype/experimental phase. Future iterations with explore necklace design and different coding variations using the Arduino.
